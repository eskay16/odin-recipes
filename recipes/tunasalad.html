Tuna salad

Home

Description

This tuna salad comes together in minutes for a delicious light meal when you are in a hurry. It's easy to make with canned tuna, creamy salad dressing, and relish. Serve in a sandwich or with crackers. It tastes great every time!

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • ¼ cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish, or to taste

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Mash tuna together with creamy salad dressing and relish in a small bowl with a fork.
